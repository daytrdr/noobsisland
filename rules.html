We want everyone to have fun

We have simple rules designed to have everyone enjoy a great experience. Our server is designed to get you started and learning the game.

  • PVE/PVP areas (No killing each other in PVE) or Jail.
  • Admins will not refund fines
  • Admins will not teleport you.
  • NO lock picking of bases and vehicles in PVE
  • Base Damage and Vehicle Damage is Disabled
  • Admins are here to oversee gameplay while also playing and having fun. Please follow by the rules and enjoy the server.
  • No promoting other servers or discords in the game or discord. That will get you booted.
  • Please no cheating, no mods whatsoever. That will get you banned.
  • 1 Flag per Squad
  • While Profanity is allowed in the server. However racist or inappropriate comments will not be tolerated.
  • 1 base only! No multiple bases! If you’re in a squad and they have a base then that is your base also, that is your 1 base.
  • Do not build in POI areas!!!
  • Please lock your house and your car and chests. When these items are unlocked they are subject to theft and we cannot control this.
  • Players are limited to 1 Land, 1 Water and 1 Air vehicle per player and a dirt bike.
  • Trader Funds are unlimited.
  • We will not wipe the server for any reason other than performance issues.
  • A violation of any of these rules could result in disciplinary action.
  • Vehicles or boats will not be replaced. Admins will not spawn vehicles.
  • If it does not have a lock…it’s free for all!! You must lock your things that can be locked.
