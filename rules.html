We want everyone to have fun

We created this server and called it Noobs Island because we wanted to create a place for new players to come learn the game, and we also encourage seasoned players too. Our Admins are friendly, and welcoming to everyone. We encourage everyone to join the Discord where we can help you better. It's too much for the Admins to type all the recommendations in game. So, please consider joining the Discord for a better learning experience. Another great way to survive is to invite your friends and SQUAD UP. Work as a team and you will find a much more fun experience.

Server rules

  1. This is a PVE Map. No base raiding.
  2. Admins will not refund fines.
  3. Admins will not teleport you.
  4. NO lock picking of bases and vehicles
  5. Base Damage and Vehicle Damage is Disabled
  6. Admins are here to oversee gameplay while also playing and having fun. Please follow by the rules and enjoy the server.
  7. No promoting other servers or discords in the game or discord. That will get you booted.
  8. Please no cheating, no mods whatsoever. That will get you banned.
  9. 1 Flag per Squad
10. While Profanity is allowed in the server. However racist or inappropriate comments will not be tolerated.
11. 1 base only! No multiple bases! If you’re in a squad and they have a base then that is your base also, that is your 1 base.
12. Do not build in POI areas!!!
13. Please lock your house and your car and chests. When these items are unlocked they are subject to theft and we cannot control this.
14. Players are limited to 1 Land, 1 Water and 1 Air vehicle per player and a dirt bike.
15. Trader Funds are unlimited.
16. We will not wipe the server for any reason other than performance issues.
17. A violation of any of these rules could result in disciplinary action.
18. Vehicles or boats will not be replaced. Admins will not spawn vehicles.
19. If it does not have a lock…it’s free for all.
